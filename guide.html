📘 Tracker Guide

🐺 Welcome to the DOW Wolves – Chest Tracker Guide!
This tracker helps us monitor clan performance during the Triumphal Challenge (TC) cycle. It's super easy to use and gives a nice overview of how we're all doing 💪.

The data is manually updated by our team to keep things as accurate and fair as possible.

🔍 How to Use It

  1. 📅 Select the TC Cycle
    Choose the TC cycle you want to review (e.g., March 2025 or April 2025). Each cycle shows progress during that month.
  2. 👁️ Pick a View Type
    You can explore the data in two ways:
    • 🗓️ Weekly View: Groups days into 4 weeks:
      • Week 1: Days 1–7
      • Week 2: Days 8–14
      • Week 3: Days 15–21
      • Week 4: Days 22–end of month
    • 📆 Daily View: Shows data for a specific day.
  3. ✅ Choose a Date or Week
    Once you've selected a view type, a new dropdown will appear to pick the exact day or week. The table and top contributors update automatically.

📋 Member Contributions Table

This table shows:

  • Member name
  • Number of Chests collected
  • Their Score

It's sorted alphabetically so you can quickly find your name and check your progress.

🏆 Top Contributors Section

This section highlights the Top 5 players based on:

  • Score - The highest accumulated during the week or day selected
  • Chests - The number of chests accumulated during the week or day selected

❗ No Data? No Worries!

If no data is available for the selected week or day, you’ll see a message like:

  • “There are no records for this day.”
  • “There are no records for this week.”
Note:
On desktop, this section appears on the right side.
On mobile, it moves below the table for better readability.
